# Example AI Report With Review Risks

This file is intentionally flawed. Use it to test whether `$comment-analysis-review` catches weak claims.

## Claims To Audit

1. Sleep breathing monitoring accounts for 49.9% of all overseas discussion.

2. Fitbit has the best brand reputation because its positive sentiment rate is 66.7%. Oura ranks second with 50%.

3. TikTok users are young seed users who are best targeted with trendy product positioning.

4. Sleep comfort has the highest negative rate at 52.3%, while night physiological monitoring has the second-highest negative rate at 69.6%.

5. Since CPAP users complain that masks are uncomfortable, Shokz can position its product as a comfortable CPAP replacement with medical-grade accuracy.

## Hidden Context For Review

- The 49.9% figure is actually 6,230 / 12,489 product-labeled records, not all 35,232 raw social records.
- Fitbit has only 3 comments.
- Oura has only 10 comments.
- Night physiological monitoring has 135 mentions and a 69.6% negative rate.
- Sleep comfort has 906 mentions and a 52.3% negative rate.
- CPAP is a treatment device; a consumer monitoring product should not be positioned as a treatment replacement without evidence.
